Adding an e-mail address

On this page you can add an e-mail address to your IRMA app.

Do you want to add multiple e-mail addresses to your IRMA app? Then perform these steps multiple times.

Check e-mail address

Please check your e-mail address again for correctness. Choose 'Back' to correct your e-mail address.

Adding an e-mail address

It may take a couple of minutes for the e-mail to arrive. If you do not see it, check your spam folder too. Please go through the following steps:

  1. Open your e-mail application.
  2. Open the e-mail message sent by SIDN.
  3. Open the link within the e-mail message.
  4. Go through the steps that are offered.
